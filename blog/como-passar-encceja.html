How to Pass ENCCEJA: Complete Study Plan for High School Certification

November 10, 2025 ¬∑ 12 min read

What Is ENCCEJA?

The ENCCEJA (Exame Nacional para Certificacao de Competencias de Jovens e Adultos) is a free national exam administered by INEP that allows young people and adults to obtain their elementary or high school diploma.

It's designed for people who didn't complete their education at the regular age. The exam is free, held annually, and you can pass it partially -- getting certified in some areas and retaking only the ones you didn't pass.

Exam Structure (Ensino Medio)

The ENCCEJA for high school has 4 knowledge areas + essay:

Area Subjects Questions
Linguagens e Codigos Portuguese, Literature, Foreign Language (English/Spanish), Arts, Physical Education 30
Matematica Mathematics and its Technologies 30
Ciencias da Natureza Biology, Chemistry, Physics 30
Ciencias Humanas History, Geography, Philosophy, Sociology 30
Redacao Argumentative essay (dissertative-argumentative) 1 essay

Minimum score: 100 points (out of 200, using TRI) in each area + 5.0 in the essay.

What Makes ENCCEJA Different

  • Partial approval: You can pass in some areas and retake only the ones you didn't reach the minimum
  • TRI scoring: Uses Item Response Theory, so consistency matters more than raw number of correct answers
  • Practical focus: Questions prioritize real-world application over theoretical memorization
  • Text interpretation: Many questions are based on reading and interpreting texts, graphs, and tables

Study Strategy with Spaced Repetition

Even though ENCCEJA questions focus on interpretation, you still need to know the fundamental concepts to interpret correctly. Flashcards with spaced repetition help you retain:

  • Math formulas: Percentages, basic geometry, equations, rule of three
  • Science concepts: Cell structure, chemical reactions, Newton's laws, energy
  • History facts: Key historical periods, important dates, cause-effect relationships
  • Grammar rules: Verb agreement, punctuation, text cohesion
  • Geography: Climate types, ecosystems, urbanization, economic sectors

8-Week Study Plan

Weeks 1-2: Linguagens e Codigos

  1. Portuguese: Text interpretation, grammar basics, verb agreement
  2. Literature: Main literary movements and key authors
  3. Create flashcards for grammar rules and vocabulary
  4. Practice essay writing (1 per week minimum)

Weeks 3-4: Matematica

  1. Basic operations, fractions, percentages, rule of three
  2. Basic geometry: areas, perimeters, volumes
  3. Reading graphs and tables (very common in ENCCEJA)
  4. Flashcards for formulas and problem-solving patterns

Weeks 5-6: Ciencias da Natureza

  1. Biology: Cell, genetics basics, ecology, health
  2. Chemistry: Elements, chemical reactions, acids/bases
  3. Physics: Mechanics, energy, electricity basics
  4. Flashcards for scientific concepts and processes

Weeks 7-8: Ciencias Humanas + Final Review

  1. History: Colonial Brazil, Republic, World Wars, Cold War
  2. Geography: Brazilian regions, climate, urbanization
  3. Philosophy/Sociology: Citizenship, human rights, democracy
  4. Solve past ENCCEJA exams and create flashcards from mistakes
  5. Intensive daily review in Mayinab

Essay Tips for ENCCEJA

  • Structure: Introduction (thesis) + 2 development paragraphs + conclusion (intervention proposal)
  • Repertoire: Use flashcards to memorize quotes, data, and examples for essay arguments
  • Connectives: Practice using transition words for text cohesion
  • Practice: Write at least 1 essay per week on current social topics

Como Passar no ENCCEJA: Plano de Estudos Completo

O que e o ENCCEJA?

O ENCCEJA (Exame Nacional para Certificacao de Competencias de Jovens e Adultos) e uma prova gratuita aplicada pelo INEP que permite obter o certificado de conclusao do Ensino Fundamental ou Ensino Medio.

E voltado para pessoas que nao concluiram os estudos na idade regular. A prova e gratuita, aplicada anualmente, e voce pode ser aprovado parcialmente -- recebendo certificacao em algumas areas e refazendo apenas as que nao atingiu a nota minima.

Estrutura da Prova (Ensino Medio)

O ENCCEJA para Ensino Medio tem 4 areas do conhecimento + Redacao:

Area Disciplinas Questoes
Linguagens e Codigos Portugues, Literatura, Lingua Estrangeira (Ingles/Espanhol), Artes, Educacao Fisica 30
Matematica Matematica e suas Tecnologias 30
Ciencias da Natureza Biologia, Quimica, Fisica 30
Ciencias Humanas Historia, Geografia, Filosofia, Sociologia 30
Redacao Texto dissertativo-argumentativo 1 redacao

Nota minima: 100 pontos (de 200, usando TRI) em cada area + 5,0 na Redacao.

O que Torna o ENCCEJA Diferente

  • Aprovacao parcial: Voce pode ser aprovado em algumas areas e refazer apenas as que nao atingiu o minimo
  • Nota TRI: Usa Teoria de Resposta ao Item, entao consistencia importa mais que acertar muitas questoes
  • Foco pratico: Questoes priorizam aplicacao no mundo real sobre memorizacao teorica
  • Interpretacao de texto: Muitas questoes sao baseadas em leitura e interpretacao de textos, graficos e tabelas

Estrategia de Estudo com Repeticao Espacada

Mesmo que as questoes do ENCCEJA foquem em interpretacao, voce ainda precisa conhecer os conceitos fundamentais para interpretar corretamente. Flashcards com repeticao espacada ajudam a reter:

  • Formulas de matematica: Porcentagens, geometria basica, equacoes, regra de tres
  • Conceitos de ciencias: Estrutura celular, reacoes quimicas, leis de Newton, energia
  • Fatos historicos: Periodos historicos, datas importantes, relacoes causa-efeito
  • Regras gramaticais: Concordancia verbal, pontuacao, coesao textual
  • Geografia: Tipos de clima, ecossistemas, urbanizacao, setores economicos

Plano de Estudos de 8 Semanas

Semanas 3-4: Matematica

  3. Leitura de graficos e tabelas (muito comum no ENCCEJA)
  4. Flashcards para formulas e padroes de resolucao

Semanas 5-6: Ciencias da Natureza

  1. Biologia: Celula, genetica basica, ecologia, saude
  2. Quimica: Elementos, reacoes quimicas, acidos/bases
  3. Fisica: Mecanica, energia, eletricidade basica
  4. Flashcards para conceitos e processos cientificos

Semanas 7-8: Ciencias Humanas + Revisao Final
