What Is the PRF and Why Is It So Competitive?

The Policia Rodoviaria Federal (PRF) is one of Brazil's most prestigious law enforcement agencies. The PRF patrols federal highways, combats drug trafficking, smuggling, and environmental crimes, and enforces traffic laws across the country.

The concurso PRF is one of the most competitive public exams in Brazil, attracting hundreds of thousands of candidates. The exam is typically organized by CESPE/CEBRASPE and uses the distinctive Certo/Errado (True/False) format with negative marking.

Exam Structure

  • Objective exam: 120 items in Certo/Errado format (CESPE style)
  • Negative marking: Each wrong answer cancels a correct one
  • TAF: Physical Aptitude Test (running, pull-ups, push-ups, sit-ups, swimming)
  • Psychological evaluation
  • Medical examination
  • CFP: Professional Training Course (academy phase)

CESPE/CEBRASPE Strategy

The CESPE exam format is unique and requires a specific strategy:

  • Negative marking: Only mark an answer if you're at least 70% sure. Leaving blank scores 0; wrong answers score -1.
  • Textual interpretation: CESPE questions are statements that may be correct or incorrect. Read carefully for absolutes ("always," "never") which are often wrong.
  • Literal vs. interpretive: Distinguish between questions testing literal law text vs. doctrinal interpretation.
  • Incomplete is not wrong: In CESPE, an incomplete statement is not necessarily incorrect unless it's misleading.

Flashcard Strategy for PRF

  • CTB articles: Create flashcards for key articles of the Codigo de Transito Brasileiro. Focus on infractions, penalties, and categories.
  • Crime classifications: Distinguish between crimes dolosos/culposos, tentativa, consumacao for traffic-related crimes.
  • Constitutional articles: Art. 5 (fundamental rights), Art. 144 (public security), Art. 37 (public administration).
  • Traffic violations: Flashcards for severity (leve, media, grave, gravissima), point values, and fine amounts.
  • Special legislation: Drug trafficking law (11.343/06), Disarmament Statute (10.826/03), Abuse of Authority.

12-Week Study Plan

Weeks 1-4: Foundation

  1. Legislacao de Transito: CTB completo -- infractions, penalties, driver licensing
  2. Direito Constitucional: Fundamental rights, Public Security (Art. 144)
  3. Lingua Portuguesa: Interpretation, grammar, punctuation
  4. Create flashcards daily in Mayinab (20-30 cards/day)

Weeks 5-8: Core Subjects

  1. Direito Penal: General part, Crimes against persons, Crimes against property
  2. Direito Administrativo: Principles, Administrative acts, Public agents
  3. Processo Penal: Arrest types (flagrante, preventiva, temporaria), Evidence
  4. Legislacao Especial: Drug law, Disarmament, Environmental crimes
  5. Daily review (30 min) + new cards from each session

Weeks 9-12: Review + Practice

  1. Informatica, Raciocinio Logico, remaining topics
  2. Solve past CESPE exams (PRF 2013, 2018, 2021)
  3. Practice Certo/Errado decision-making -- train your confidence threshold
  4. Create flashcards from every mistake
  5. Intensive SM-2 review in Mayinab

TAF Preparation

Don't neglect the physical component. Start training early:

  • Running: 12-minute Cooper test or timed distance
  • Pull-ups, push-ups, sit-ups: Progressive overload training
  • Swimming: 50m in the required time
  • Train at least 4x/week alongside your study sessions

O que e a PRF e Por que e Tao Concorrido?

A Policia Rodoviaria Federal (PRF) e uma das instituicoes policiais mais prestigiadas do Brasil. A PRF patrulha rodovias federais, combate trafico de drogas, contrabando e crimes ambientais, e fiscaliza o cumprimento das leis de transito em todo o pais.

O concurso da PRF e um dos mais concorridos do Brasil, atraindo centenas de milhares de candidatos. A prova e tipicamente organizada pelo CESPE/CEBRASPE e usa o formato distintivo Certo/Errado com marcacao negativa.

Estrutura do Concurso

  • Prova objetiva: 120 itens no formato Certo/Errado (estilo CESPE)
  • Marcacao negativa: Cada resposta errada anula uma certa
  • TAF: Teste de Aptidao Fisica (corrida, barra, flexao, abdominal, natacao)
  • Avaliacao psicologica
  • Exame medico
  • CFP: Curso de Formacao Profissional (fase academia)

Estrategia CESPE/CEBRASPE

O formato CESPE e unico e exige uma estrategia especifica:

  • Marcacao negativa: So marque se tiver pelo menos 70% de certeza. Deixar em branco vale 0; errar vale -1.
  • Interpretacao textual: Questoes CESPE sao afirmacoes que podem estar certas ou erradas. Cuidado com absolutos ("sempre", "nunca") que frequentemente estao errados.
  • Literal vs. interpretativo: Distinga entre questoes que testam texto literal da lei vs. interpretacao doutrinaria.
  • Incompleto nao e errado: No CESPE, uma afirmacao incompleta nao e necessariamente incorreta, a menos que seja enganosa.

Estrategia de Flashcards para PRF

  • Artigos do CTB: Crie flashcards para artigos-chave do Codigo de Transito Brasileiro. Foque em infracoes, penalidades e categorias.
  • Classificacao de crimes: Distinga crimes dolosos/culposos, tentativa, consumacao para crimes de transito.
  • Artigos constitucionais: Art. 5 (direitos fundamentais), Art. 144 (seguranca publica), Art. 37 (administracao publica).
  • Infracoes de transito: Flashcards por gravidade (leve, media, grave, gravissima), pontuacao e valores das multas.
  • Legislacao especial: Lei de Drogas (11.343/06), Estatuto do Desarmamento (10.826/03), Abuso de Autoridade.

  2. Direito Administrativo: Principios, Atos administrativos, Agentes publicos
  3. Processo Penal: Tipos de prisao (flagrante, preventiva, temporaria), Provas
  4. Legislacao Especial: Lei de Drogas, Desarmamento, Crimes ambientais
