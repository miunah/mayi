Study Smarter by Focusing on What Matters

Not all topics are equally likely to appear on your vestibular. Analysis of past exams from FUVEST, UNICAMP, UNESP, and other top schools reveals clear patterns. Biology: The Most Tested Topics

Biology is one of the heaviest subjects in most vestibulares, especially for Medicina candidates.

Topic Frequency Flashcard Strategy
Ecology Very High Food chains, biomes, ecological relationships, biogeochemical cycles
Genetics Very High Mendel's laws, Punnett squares, linked genes, inheritance patterns
Cytology High Organelles, membrane transport, cell division (mitosis/meiosis)
Human Physiology High Nervous, circulatory, digestive, and endocrine systems
Evolution Medium-High Natural selection, Darwin vs Lamarck, speciation, evidence
Biochemistry Medium Photosynthesis, cellular respiration, enzymes, macromolecules

Mathematics: The Most Tested Topics

Topic Frequency Flashcard Strategy
Functions Very High Quadratic, exponential, logarithmic. Use LaTeX for graphs/formulas.
Ratio & Proportion Very High Rule of three, percentages, scale problems. Include worked examples.
Geometry High Plane and spatial geometry formulas. Areas, volumes, Pythagorean theorem.
Statistics & Probability High Mean, median, mode, standard deviation. Probability rules.
Trigonometry Medium-High Unit circle, sine/cosine/tangent, identities. LaTeX essential.
Combinatorics Medium Permutations, combinations, counting principles.

Tip: Always include the formula AND an example problem on your flashcards. Understanding when to apply a formula is as important as knowing it.

Physics: The Most Tested Topics

Topic Frequency Flashcard Strategy
Mechanics Very High Newton's laws, kinematics, energy, momentum. Core formulas with LaTeX.
Electricity High Ohm's law, circuits, electric potential, capacitors.
Thermodynamics High Heat transfer, gas laws, thermal expansion, entropy.
Optics Medium-High Mirrors, lenses, refraction, diffraction. Include ray diagrams.
Waves Medium Wave equation, sound, electromagnetic spectrum.

Chemistry: The Most Tested Topics

Topic Frequency Flashcard Strategy
Organic Chemistry Very High Functional groups, nomenclature (IUPAC), isomerism, main reactions.
Stoichiometry Very High Molar mass, balancing equations, yield calculations. LaTeX for formulas.
Chemical Equilibrium High Le Chatelier's principle, Kc/Kp, pH, buffers.
Electrochemistry High Redox reactions, galvanic cells, electrolysis, electrode potentials.
Thermochemistry Medium-High Hess's law, enthalpy, entropy, free energy.

History: The Most Tested Topics

Topic Frequency Flashcard Strategy
Colonial Brazil Very High Economic cycles, slavery, colonial administration. Cause-effect cards.
Vargas Era Very High 1930 Revolution, Estado Novo, labor laws, industrialization.
Military Dictatorship High AI-5, economic miracle, political repression, redemocratization.
French Revolution High Causes, key events, phases, consequences for Latin America.
World Wars High Causes, alliances, key battles, consequences, Treaty of Versailles.
Cold War Medium-High Capitalism vs communism, proxy wars, space race, fall of Berlin Wall.

Geography: The Most Tested Topics

Topic Frequency Flashcard Strategy
Urbanization Very High Rural exodus, favelas, gentrification, urban planning.
Environment Very High Deforestation, climate change, sustainability, biomes.
Geopolitics High Globalization, trade blocs, international conflicts, migration.
Brazilian Geography High Regions, economic activity, demographic data, natural resources.
Cartography Medium Map projections, scale, coordinates, time zones.

Portuguese & Literature: The Most Tested Topics

Topic Frequency Flashcard Strategy
Text Interpretation Very High Rhetorical devices, argumentation types, text genres.
Mandatory Reading Very High (FUVEST/UNICAMP) One topic per book: characters, themes, historical context, style.
Grammar High Verbal agreement, punctuation, pronouns, syntax.
Literary Movements High Romanticism, Realism, Modernism, key authors per movement.
