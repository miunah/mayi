What Is the IBGE and Why Take the Concurso?

The IBGE (Instituto Brasileiro de Geografia e Estatistica) is Brazil's main statistical and geographic agency, responsible for the national census, economic surveys, and geographic mapping. IBGE concursos offer positions across Brazil and are considered excellent entry-level public service opportunities.

The IBGE regularly opens large-scale selection processes with thousands of positions, making it one of the most accessible concursos in Brazil.

Available Positions

Position Education Level Key Responsibilities
Recenseador Fundamental (Elementary) Home visits for census data collection, form filling, community engagement
Agente Censitario Supervisor (ACS) Medio (High School) Supervise census teams, quality control, report management
Agente Censitario Municipal (ACM) Medio (High School) Coordinate census operations at municipal level, logistics
Analista Censitario Superior (Bachelor's) Data analysis, methodology, research design, IT systems

Exam Structure

Recenseador (Elementary Level)

  • Lingua Portuguesa: Text interpretation, grammar basics
  • Matematica: Basic operations, percentages, graph reading
  • Conhecimentos Especificos: Ethics, IBGE functions, census methodology
  • 50 multiple choice questions, 3 hours

Agente Censitario (High School Level)

  • Lingua Portuguesa: Interpretation, grammar, text production
  • Raciocinio Logico: Logic, sets, sequences, proportions
  • Conhecimentos Especificos: Administration, geography, census methodology, ethics
  • Informatica: Office tools, operating systems, internet
  • 60 multiple choice questions, 3.5 hours

Analista Censitario (Bachelor's Level)

  • All subjects above + specific area knowledge
  • Statistics, data analysis, research methodology
  • 70 multiple choice questions + discursive, 4 hours

Sample Flashcards for IBGE

  • Q: What is PNAD? A: Pesquisa Nacional por Amostra de Domicilios -- a household survey that collects socioeconomic data on population, education, work, income, and housing
  • Q: What are the 5 regions of Brazil? A: Norte, Nordeste, Centro-Oeste, Sudeste, Sul
  • Q: What is the difference between population and sample? A: Population is the complete set of elements; sample is a representative subset used for statistical inference
  • Q: What are the principles of public service ethics (Decree 1.171/94)? A: Dignity, decorum, zeal, efficiency, public interest, morality, legality, impersonality, publicity

O que e o IBGE e Por que Fazer o Concurso?

O IBGE (Instituto Brasileiro de Geografia e Estatistica) e o principal orgao de pesquisa estatistica e geografica do Brasil, responsavel pelo censo nacional, pesquisas economicas e mapeamento geografico. Os concursos do IBGE oferecem vagas em todo o Brasil e sao considerados excelentes portas de entrada no servico publico.

O IBGE abre regularmente processos seletivos de grande escala com milhares de vagas, tornando-o um dos concursos mais acessiveis do Brasil.
