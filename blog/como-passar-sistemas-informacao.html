Why Sistemas de Informacao Is a Smart and Competitive Choice

Sistemas de Informacao (Information Systems) is one of the fastest-growing tech courses in Brazil. At USP (EACH), UNICAMP, UFMG, UFRJ, and top private universities, competition has been rising steadily as the tech industry demands more professionals.

Unlike Computer Science (which is heavily theoretical), SI blends technology, business management, and applied problem-solving. The vestibular focuses on Mathematics, Portuguese, and general knowledge -- but don't be fooled by the apparent simplicity. At top universities, the cutoff scores are high and the margin for error is thin.

This guide shows you how to build a study plan that maximizes retention using spaced repetition and flashcards with Mayinab.

The 3 Pillars of SI Vestibular Preparation

1. Mathematics: The Foundation of Tech

Math is the highest-weight subject for SI in most vestibulares and ENEM:

  • Functions: Linear, quadratic, exponential, logarithmic -- graphs, domains, transformations
  • Statistics & Probability: Mean, median, mode, standard deviation, combinatorics, conditional probability
  • Logic: Propositional logic, truth tables, quantifiers, logical equivalences
  • Geometry: Areas, volumes, analytic geometry basics, coordinate plane
  • Number Theory: Divisibility, prime numbers, modular arithmetic
  • Financial Math: Simple and compound interest, percentage, ratio and proportion

Create flashcards with LaTeX formulas in Mayinab for each type of function, probability formula, and logic rule. SM-2 ensures you recall them instantly on exam day.

2. Portuguese & Essay: Your Competitive Edge

Portuguese and Essay (Redacao) often carry more weight than candidates expect:

  • Text Interpretation: Reading comprehension, inference, author's intent, textual genres
  • Grammar: Syntax, morphology, verbal regency, concordance, punctuation
  • Essay (ENEM): The 5 competencies, argumentative structure, social intervention proposal
  • Literature: Required readings for FUVEST/UNICAMP, literary movements

Use Mayinab to build flashcards for grammar rules, literary works summaries, and essay repertoire. Weekly review with SM-2 keeps everything fresh.

3. General Knowledge: Sciences & Humanities

For ENEM and most vestibulares, you also need:

  • Physics: Mechanics basics, electricity (circuits, Ohm's law), thermodynamics fundamentals
  • Chemistry: Stoichiometry, organic chemistry basics, thermochemistry
  • History & Geography: Brazilian history, globalization, urbanization, current events
  • IT & Digital Culture: Some vestibulares test basic concepts of computing, internet, and data

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Underestimating the vestibular: SI at top universities has high cutoff scores. Prepare seriously.
  • Ignoring Portuguese and Essay: Many tech-oriented students neglect languages. Redacao can be decisive at ENEM.
  • Not practicing logic: Logic is both a Math topic and a foundation of SI. Practice truth tables and propositions.
  • Studying passively: Reading notes without testing yourself leads to forgetting 80% within a week.
  • Skipping past exams: Past questions reveal patterns. Solve them regularly and create flashcards for mistakes.

Os 3 Pilares da Preparacao para SI

Matematica e a materia de maior peso para SI na maioria dos vestibulares e no ENEM:

  • Geometria: Areas, volumes, nocoes de geometria analitica, plano cartesiano

Portugues e Redacao costumam ter mais peso do que os candidatos esperam:

  • Quimica: Estequiometria, nocoes de quimica organica, termoquimica
  • Historia e Geografia: Historia do Brasil, globalizacao, urbanizacao, atualidades
