Subject-by-Subject Strategy

1. Portuguese Language (Highest Weight)

  • Text interpretation: Main idea, inference, implicit meaning, vocabulary in context
  • Grammar: Concordancia verbal/nominal, regencia, crase, pontuacao
  • Spelling and accentuation: The new orthographic agreement rules
  • Text cohesion: Connectors, conjunctions, reference terms
  • Official writing: Oficio, memorando, requerimento formats (for Atendente)

2. Mathematics

  • Basic operations: Fractions, decimals, percentages
  • Rule of three: Simple and compound
  • Areas and perimeters: Rectangle, circle, triangle
  • Proportional reasoning: Ratios, direct and inverse proportion
  • Basic statistics: Mean, median, mode, simple graphs
  • Unit conversions: Weight (grams/kilos), distance (km), time

3. Information Technology Basics

  • Operating systems: Windows 10/11 basics, file management
  • Office tools: Word (formatting, tables), Excel (basic formulas, SUM, AVERAGE)
  • Internet: Browsers, email, search, cloud basics
  • Information security: Viruses, phishing, passwords, safe browsing

4. Correios Knowledge

  • Products: SEDEX, PAC, Correios Mini Envios, Carta Registrada
  • Services: Logistics, international mail, e-commerce solutions
  • CEP system: How ZIP codes work in Brazil
  • History: Brief history and mission of Correios
  • Regulations: Postal regulations and customer service standards

5. General Knowledge & Current Events

  • Brazilian geography: Regions, states, major cities
  • Current affairs: Government programs, economy, technology trends
  • Ethics and citizenship: Basic civic concepts
