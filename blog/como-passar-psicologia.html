How to Pass Vestibular de Psicologia: Complete Study Plan

December 17, 2025 ¬∑ 13 min read

Why Psicologia Is One of the Most Competitive Courses

Psicologia (Psychology) is one of the most sought-after courses in Brazilian universities. At USP, the candidate-per-seat ratio regularly exceeds 30:1. At UNICAMP, UNESP, UFMG, and PUC-SP, the competition is equally intense.

Psychology demands a unique combination of biological sciences and humanities. You need to master Biology (especially neuroscience and physiology), while also excelling in History, Philosophy, Sociology, and Essay writing. This dual demand means you must retain a wide range of concepts across very different disciplines.

This guide shows you how to build a study plan that maximizes retention using spaced repetition and flashcards with Mayinab.

The 3 Pillars of Psychology Vestibular Preparation

1. Biology: The Scientific Foundation

Biology is the highest-weight subject for Psychology, especially topics related to human behavior and the brain:

  • Neuroscience: Neurons, synapses, neurotransmitters, central and peripheral nervous system
  • Human Physiology: Endocrine system (hormones and behavior), sensory systems, homeostasis
  • Genetics: Heredity, gene expression, nature vs. nurture debate
  • Evolution: Natural selection, evolutionary psychology concepts, human evolution
  • Ecology: Population dynamics, human impact, biomes
  • Cytology: Cell structure, membrane transport, cell signaling

Create detailed flashcards for each biological process. For example: "How do neurotransmitters cross the synaptic cleft and what happens at the postsynaptic receptor?" This builds deep understanding, not surface-level memorization.

2. Humanities: History, Philosophy & Sociology

Humanities carry enormous weight in Psychology vestibulares, especially at USP and UNICAMP:

  • Philosophy: Ethics, philosophy of mind, empiricism vs. rationalism, existentialism, phenomenology
  • History: Modern and contemporary history, social movements, Brazilian history and culture
  • Sociology: Durkheim, Weber, Marx, social institutions, inequality, identity
  • Geography: Urbanization, demographic transitions, globalization, social geography

Use Mayinab to create flashcards linking philosophers to their key ideas: "Descartes -- mind-body dualism," "Freud -- unconscious, id/ego/superego." The SM-2 algorithm ensures you won't forget these connections.

3. Essay (Redacao): Your Competitive Edge

The essay can make or break your score. For Psychology specifically:

  • Argumentative structure: Thesis, evidence, counterargument, conclusion
  • Social themes: Mental health, education, inequality, ethics in science
  • Repertoire: Philosophical and sociological references (Foucault, Hannah Arendt, Bauman)
  • ENEM competencies: The 5 competencies that define your essay score

Create flashcards with essay repertoire: quotes, authors, and social data you can cite. Review them weekly with SM-2 so they're always fresh.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Neglecting Biology: Many Psychology candidates focus only on Humanities. Biology has the highest weight -- don't skip it.
  • Only reading without testing: Passive reading doesn't create lasting memory. Always test yourself with flashcards.
  • Ignoring Philosophy: Philosophy of mind and ethics are directly relevant to Psychology and appear frequently in vestibulares.
  • Weak essay repertoire: Without solid references (authors, data, quotes), your essay will lack depth. Build repertoire cards early.
  • Starting too late: Psicologia is competitive. 8-10 months of consistent study is the minimum for top universities.

Por que Psicologia E um dos Cursos Mais Concorridos

Psicologia e um dos cursos mais disputados nas universidades brasileiras. Na USP, UNICAMP, UNESP, UFMG e PUC-SP, a concorrencia e altissima.

Psicologia exige uma combinacao unica de ciencias biologicas e humanas. Voce precisa dominar Biologia (especialmente neurociencia e fisiologia), enquanto tambem se destaca em Historia, Filosofia, Sociologia e Redacao. Essa dupla exigencia significa que voce deve reter uma vasta gama de conceitos em disciplinas muito diferentes.

Os 3 Pilares da Preparacao para Psicologia

1. Biologia: A Base Cientifica

Biologia e a materia de maior peso para Psicologia, especialmente topicos relacionados ao comportamento humano e ao cerebro:

  • Neurociencia: Neuronios, sinapses, neurotransmissores, sistema nervoso central e periferico
  • Fisiologia Humana: Sistema endocrino (hormonios e comportamento), sistemas sensoriais, homeostase
  • Genetica: Hereditariedade, expressao genica, debate natureza vs. criacao
  • Evolucao: Selecao natural, conceitos de psicologia evolutiva, evolucao humana
  • Ecologia: Dinamica populacional, impacto humano, biomas
  • Citologia: Estrutura celular, transporte de membrana, sinalizacao celular

Crie flashcards detalhados para cada processo biologico. Por exemplo: "Como os neurotransmissores cruzam a fenda sinaptica e o que acontece no receptor pos-sinaptico?" Isso constroi compreensao profunda, nao memorizacao superficial.

2. Humanas: Historia, Filosofia e Sociologia

Humanas tem peso enorme nos vestibulares de Psicologia, especialmente na USP e UNICAMP:

  • Filosofia: Etica, filosofia da mente, empirismo vs. racionalismo, existencialismo, fenomenologia
  • Historia: Historia moderna e contemporanea, movimentos sociais, historia e cultura brasileira
  • Sociologia: Durkheim, Weber, Marx, instituicoes sociais, desigualdade, identidade
  • Geografia: Urbanizacao, transicoes demograficas, globalizacao, geografia social

Use o Mayinab para criar flashcards ligando filosofos as suas ideias-chave: "Descartes -- dualismo mente-corpo," "Freud -- inconsciente, id/ego/superego." O algoritmo SM-2 garante que voce nao esqueca essas conexoes.

3. Redacao: Seu Diferencial Competitivo

A redacao pode definir sua aprovacao. Para Psicologia especificamente:

  • Estrutura argumentativa: Tese, evidencia, contra-argumento, conclusao
  • Temas sociais: Saude mental, educacao, desigualdade, etica na ciencia
  • Repertorio: Referencias filosoficas e sociologicas (Foucault, Hannah Arendt, Bauman)
  • Competencias do ENEM: As 5 competencias que definem sua nota na redacao

Crie flashcards com repertorio para redacao: citacoes, autores e dados sociais que voce pode citar. Revise semanalmente com SM-2 para que estejam sempre frescos.
