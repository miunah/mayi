What Is Banco do Brasil?

Banco do Brasil (BB) is Brazil's oldest and largest bank by assets, and one of the biggest in Latin America. It's a mixed-economy company (government-controlled but publicly traded) with thousands of branches across all Brazilian states and international presence.

BB is a leader in rural credit (agribusiness financing), corporate banking, and government services. Working at BB offers:

  • Job stability: Public employee protections after probation period
  • Competitive salary: Attractive compensation with benefits (check the latest edital for updated values)
  • 30-hour workweek: Standard banking hours with better work-life balance
  • Strong benefits: Health/dental insurance, meal allowance, PLR, pension, education support
  • Career growth: Internal promotions with clear path to management and executive roles
  • National presence: Work in any state, with transfer possibilities

The core subject for any banking concurso:

  • Exchange & trade finance: Export/import operations, ACC, ACE, letters of credit
  • Compliance: PLD/FT, KYC/KYP, COAF, insider trading, suitability
