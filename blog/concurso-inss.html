What Is INSS?

The INSS (Instituto Nacional do Seguro Social) is Brazil's social security agency, responsible for managing pensions, disability benefits, maternity leave, death benefits, and all social insurance programs for millions of beneficiaries. It's one of the largest public agencies in Brazil with offices nationwide.

Working at INSS offers:

  • Job stability: Federal public servant with lifetime tenure after probation
  • Good salary: Attractive compensation + benefits (check the latest edital for updated values)
  • Positions everywhere: INSS offices in every region of Brazil
  • Social impact: Help millions of Brazilians access retirement and benefits
  • Accessible entry: Tecnico requires only high school diploma
  • High number of vacancies: INSS concursos typically open many positions

Subject-by-Subject Strategy

1. Social Security Law (Direito Previdenciario) -- THE Key Subject

This is the most important subject, representing 30-40% of the exam:

  • RGPS (Regime Geral): Who is covered, mandatory vs. optional insured, dependents
  • Benefits: All types of benefits with requirements:
    • Aposentadoria por idade (age retirement)
    • Aposentadoria por tempo de contribuicao (contribution time retirement)
    • Aposentadoria por invalidez (disability retirement)
    • Auxilio-doenca (sickness benefit)
    • Auxilio-acidente (accident benefit)
    • Salario-maternidade (maternity pay)
    • Salario-familia (family allowance)
    • Pensao por morte (death pension)
    • Auxilio-reclusao (incarceration benefit)
  • Contribution periods: Carencia (waiting period) for each benefit
  • Calculation: Salary-de-beneficio, Renda Mensal Inicial (RMI)
  • Special rules: Rural workers, fishermen, special retirement (harmful conditions)
  • Pension reform (EC 103/2019): New rules, transition rules, minimum age
  • Key laws: Lei 8.213/91 (Benefits Plan), Lei 8.212/91 (Financing), Decreto 3.048/99 (Regulation)

2. Constitutional Law

  • Social security in the Constitution: Arts. 194-204 (Social Security, Health, Social Assistance)
  • Fundamental rights (Art. 5): Core incisos
  • Public administration (Art. 37): LIMPE principles
  • Social rights (Art. 6-7): Labor and social protection

3. Administrative Law

  • Federal public servants (Lei 8.112/90): Rights, duties, disciplinary process
  • Administrative process (Lei 9.784/99): Principles, phases, appeals
  • Administrative acts: Elements, attributes, invalidation
  • Powers of administration: Disciplinary, hierarchical, police

4. Portuguese Language

  • Text interpretation: CESPE style -- inferences, implicit meaning
  • Grammar: Concordancia, regencia, crase, pronoun placement
  • Text rewriting: Common in CESPE -- maintain meaning with different structure

5. Logical Reasoning

  • Propositional logic: Connectors, truth tables, negation
  • Arguments: Validity, syllogisms
  • Sets and counting: Venn diagrams, inclusion-exclusion
